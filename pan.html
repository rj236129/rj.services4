PAN Card

What is PAN?
PAN (Permanent Account Number) is a unique 10-digit code required for financial transactions, like filing taxes, opening bank accounts, etc.

Why PAN?
It's needed for all major financial activities like investments, property transactions, and more.

Who can apply?
Anyone in India, including individuals and businesses, can apply for a PAN card.
